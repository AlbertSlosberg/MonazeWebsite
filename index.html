Monaze - the breath of modern in vintage.

With our vintage color palette the strap of your watch will look timeless in combination with our modern customizable clock faces..

  • Our brand gives you the opportunity to customize your own watch.
  •  Vintage is the main style that remains through out all collections. 
  • Bespoken  item always emphisizes your individuality.     

In a world of conformity we believe that individuality should be charised, only then your unique personality will stand out. While everyone walks around wearing and using the same products, our brand offers you the chance to make your product personal, different and unique. Nowadays in a world of mass production our brand makes you stand out: every item produced is     modern,  bespoke  and  vintage.

Why us 

Our Brand Values 

01

Uniqueness 

We want people to be their true unique selves, so they don’t hide behind bland colours and blend in with the crowd.  

02

Self-expression  

By offering customizable options, we allow the consumer to express their personality through our product.  

03

Bespoke 

Monaze is against mass production and fast fashion. Therefore, we produce a certain amount of our products, which ensures high quality and care put into them.
